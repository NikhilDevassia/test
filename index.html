Pay After Getting Hired
With our ISA (pay after placement) plan, you pay the course fee
only after getting hired above the committed salary. When you
are hired, you share 20% of your monthly salary until the total
course fee is paid.

How our ISA works

  • Pay ₹0 tuition fee until you land a high paying job.
  • Pay 20% of your salary until the course fee is paid.
  • Pay nothing if you don’t get a job within 12 months of course completion.
  • If you loose your job, we help you find another.
  • If you decide to leave the program before finishing, you pay:
    During Pre-Course Nothing
    Within 30 days 50k
    Within 31-60 days 1 Lac
  • You are required to report any change in your income/job
